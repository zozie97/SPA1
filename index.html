Internet Infrastructure in Wellington : A Description


This website is about Internet Infrastructure around the city of Wellington, New Zealand. Internet Infrastructure is everything that brings the internet alive. From the servers, networks and cables to security cameras and wifi routers.


Main Infrastructure in Wellington

Datacom Servers

Datacom is one place in Wellington that is a main data centre. A data centre is a place that holds a networks main servers and systems. They are crutial so that daily operations run smoothly.

Chorus Engineering

Chorus boxes like these can be found scattered all over the city. The are where chorus connects cables from your house to the main fibre cables that carry the internet.

United Networks

This united networks cover is where the cables join together. It is a place where engineers can fix the cables if needed or check on them.

Power Box

The power box / transformer is a hub for the main cables that carry the internet to and from different areas.

Markings

The markings on the ground indicate where different cables are located and what kind of cables they are for, for example Internet Cables or Power Cables.

Infrastructure Accessories in Wellington

WiFi Router

Wifi Routers are one of the last steps to get the internet from the network servers to your own personal device such as your computer or cellphone. It transmits the internet signal from the cable, wirelessly to your device.

Traffic Lights

The use of Internet Infrastructure helps to control traffic lights. It controls how long each light goes for and when to turn on and off the cross walk lights. It also means that if there is a malfunction with the traffic lights, it will send a signal back through the roadside control connection box and through the internet to the transport agency running the traffic lights to alert them. Traffic lights are a vital part of the way we get around Wellington and so they are an important outcome from the Internet Infrastructure.

Security Cameras

Security cameras are a lot like traffic lights, the internet infrastructure helps to control them. The camera feed is relayed back through the internet to the security headquarters.
